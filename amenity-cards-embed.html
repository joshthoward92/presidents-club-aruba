The Resort

Everything You Need.
Nothing You Don't.

Set on 12 lush acres directly on the white sands of Palm Beach, the Hyatt Regency Aruba is the most refined full-service resort on the island. From the moment you arrive, every detail is handled — the beach is steps away, the pool is calling, and the only thing left to decide is whether you start at the spa or the swim-up bar.

🏊
Two Pools, One Decision
Choose the 8,000 sq. ft. Activity Pool — two-story waterslide, waterfalls, and a swim-up bar — or the Trankilo adults-only pool for something more serene.
🌊
Private Palm Beach
Step directly from the resort onto one of the most celebrated beaches in the Caribbean. Beach palapas, watersports, and cabana rentals all steps from your door.
💆
ZoiA Spa
Aruba's premier resort spa — massages, facials, and body treatments using locally sourced ingredients. Specialty treatments available poolside at Trankilo.
🍽️
Four Restaurants On-Site
From open-air seafood at Ruinas del Mar to beachfront bites at Palms Restaurant to poolside drinks at Balashi Bar & Grill — something great, always within reach.
🎰
Casino & Nightlife
The 11,200 sq. ft. casino features slots and table games. Palm Beach's entertainment strip is just minutes away on foot.
🏄
Activities On Demand
Daily yoga, mixology classes, tennis, scuba diving, catamaran sunset cruises, snorkeling, kayaking — and an Adventure Concierge team to book it all for you.
